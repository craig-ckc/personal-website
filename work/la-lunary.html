La Lunary

Escape the ordinary

The challenge

La Lunary is a South African retail establishment designed for fans of anime and Japanese culture. La Lunary's founders, who are anime enthusiasts themselves, wanted to create a single destination where weebs and non-weebs in South Africa could purchase the hottest and best products of all things Japanese lifestyle and culture. It was my responsibility to create a look that is “fun and speaks to the weebs”.

Brand Rebuild

The first task was to give a makeover to La Lunary’s logotype and identity.

Fun fact, the symbol is the Japanese character for La.

Design for People

Good site interactivity is a really, really big deal. It’s more than aesthetics and functionality because it dictates user connection. Our goal was to create a site that scales while considering growth and emphasizing building brand loyalty rather than just selling products.

The website allows the creation of customised orders with instant 3D preview.

Web version adapted for smartphones.

Not just another online store

We 100% met our goal La Lunary's site reflects a product that includes satisfying interaction and a voice that speaks to weebs.
