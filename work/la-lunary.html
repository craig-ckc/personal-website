La Lunary

Escape the ordinary

The challenge

La Lunary is a South African retail experience created for fans of all things anime and Japanese culture. Being fans themselves, the people at La Lunary wanted to provide a single location where all South Africans and others beyond, weebs and non-weebs, could get the hottest and best cosplay costume and merchandise for anime. My task was to create a style that aligns with these goals and “speaks to the weebs”.
