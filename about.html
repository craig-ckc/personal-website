Me and my work

Open introvert with a passion for design

My goal

As a designer, my goal is to tell a story through the work I create. I have helped my clients find exceptional solutions for their businesses, creating memorable brands and digital products, in short, incredible experiences. My expertise grows each day, and with that experience, I can help develop better experiences.

Design that goes beyond design

Good design is not just about meeting the expectations of usability, utility, or aesthetics. I'm always considering the best way to make art more collaborative, creative, inclusive, and impactful. I love simplifying a problem's complexity and using creative intelligence to develop an elegant solution.

Spotlight? No, thanks. Because it's not just the work; it's the passion. It's not just clients; it's people. Every project I take on is one I am excited about and believe in. Design is ultimately just a tool for giving people incredible experiences.

My design process for everything

Research &
Discovery

During this phase, I work with you to define your goals, identify the challenges in the way, and consider the user pain points. I achieve this by asking questions and challenging your assumptions to uncover the most meaningful insights, which we'll package going forward.

Strategy &
Planning

Here’s where we take every insight learned through research to establish an actionable communication guide. This will be the foundation from which to build the design. Working with the same baseline, we can get consistent and positive results throughout the process.

Ideation &
Prototyping

This is where we start working on the actual physical representation of our design by exploring wider before we go deeper. The main objective is to get any and all ideas out of the head onto paper and quickly prototype them. This will help us avoid overlooking anything.

Design &
Development

With everything set up, we can start with the design that the user interacts with. The better the experience of the interaction, the more users it attracts. I try to avoid cookie-cutter solutions, creating an individualized design for your unique audience and needs.
